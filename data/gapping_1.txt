John-un chayk-ul ___ , Mary-nun sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
----
John-un chayk-ul ilk-ess-ko , Mary-nun sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
# John-TOP book-ACC read-and Mary-TOP newspaper-ACC read-PAST-IND
# ‘John read a book and Mary read a newspaper.’
# (Ø = ilk-ess-ko ‘read-and’)

John-un chayk-ul ___ , kuliko Mary-nun sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
----
John-un chayk-ul ilk-ess-ta , kuliko Mary-nun sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
# John-TOP book-ACC read and Mary-TOP newspaper-ACC read-PAST-IND
# ‘John read a book and Mary read a newspaper.’
# (Ø = ilk-ess-ta ‘read’)

John-un yelshimhi chayk-ul ___ , Mary-nun coyonghi sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
----
John-un yelshimhi chayk-ul ilk-ess-ko , Mary-nun coyonghi sinmwun-ul ilk-ess-ta .
# John-TOP earnestly book-ACC read-and Mary-TOP quietly newspaper-ACC read-PAST-IND
# ‘John read a book earnestly and Mary read a newspaper quietly.’
# (Ø = ilk-ess-ko ‘read-and’)

Anna-nun caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Mary-nun ___ ha-n-ta .
----
Anna-nun caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Mary-nun caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak ha-n-ta
#'Anna doesn't think she is tall. Mary does.'


Chelswu-ka caki-uy phyenci-lul peli-ess-ta . Yengswu-to ___ kura-yss-ta .
----
Chelswu-ka caki-uy phyenci-lul peli-ess-ta . Yengswu-to caki-uy phyenci-lul peli-ess-ta .
#‘Chelswu1 threw out his letter. Yengsw did too.’


John-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Bill-un ___ ha-n-ta .
----
John-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Bill-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak ha-n-ta .
#‘John doesn’t think that he is tall. Bill does.’

John-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Bill-to ___ an-ha-n-ta .
----
John-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Bill-un caki-ka khu-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta .
#‘John doesn’t think that he is tall. Bill also doesn't.’


John-un Mary-ka chakha-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Tom-do ___ kureh-ta .
----
John-un Mary-ka chakha-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta . Tom-do Mary-ka chakha-ta-ko sayngkak an-ha-n-ta .
#‘John doesn’t think that Mary is nice. Tom also doesn't think that Mary is nice.’


Simcie, nay-ka ___ ha-n kes-pota-to te manhun nonmwun-ul Mary-nun ssu-ess-ta .
----
Simcie, nay-ka nonmwun-ul ssu-n kes-pota-to te manhun nonmwun-ul Mary-nun ssu-ess-ta .
#‘Mary wrote more papers than I even did(wrote).’


John-un caki-uy kay-wa kotcal sanpo-lul ha-n-ta . Kulena Bill-un ___ an-i-ta .
----
John-un caki-uy kay-wa kotcal sanpo-lul ha-n-ta . Kulena Bill-un caki-uy kay-wa kotcal sanpo-lul an-ha-n-ta.
'John often takes a walk with his dog. But Bill doesn't.'
